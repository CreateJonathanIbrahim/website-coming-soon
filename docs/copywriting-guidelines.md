## ✍️ Writing Voice & Tone

**The Vibe:** Authoritative expert on the surface, unexpected warmth and self-awareness underneath. Direct, frank, real. Unconventional, non-corporate.

### Structural Patterns

- Short punchy opener, followed by explanatory follow-up.
- Em dashes used liberally for asides and rhythm.
- First-person, present tense, conversational.
- Specific numbers/concrete details over vague claims.
- Self-aware admissions ("It's closer to a compulsion.") and character-revealing humor.
- Pull quotes to crystallize core insights.

### 🚫 STRICT "DO NOT USE" LIST

- **Corporate buzzwords:** "passionate about", "results-driven", "synergy", "leverage", "ecosystem".
- **Passive voice.**
- **Filler adjectives:** incredible, amazing, transformative.
- **Vague claims** without specific metrics.
- **LinkedIn-speak:** Anything that sounds like it was written by an HR committee.

**The Litmus Test:** Would a real, smart, slightly unconventional professional say this out loud over a beer? If it sounds like a press release, rewrite it.
