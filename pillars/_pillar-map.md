# Pillar Map

Six thematic territories. Every source and every piece of content belongs to at least one. When briefing content, identify the pillar(s) first. They determine which source files to load and what Jonathan's specific angle is.

| Pillar | File | Territory |
|--------|------|-----------|
| Project Management Systems | `project-management-systems.md` | PM as a discipline at agency scale. Coordination across functions, scope as a system, client communication as operational decisions, AI-assisted PM, EOS leadership. Tool-agnostic (ClickUp specifics live in their own pillar). |
| Digital Marketing Operations | `digital-marketing-operations.md` | How digital marketing actually gets done. Content production at scale, deliverable workflows, quality gates, service-business website strategy, the operations layer beneath what gets called "digital marketing." |
| Claude Code Architecture & Practical AI Tooling | `claude-code-architecture.md` | Claude Code as a platform (skills, hooks, rules, CLAUDE.md, layer hierarchy, context management) plus the orchestration layer around it (Claude API, Make.com, n8n, Zapier, MCP servers). Where AI gets built into actual delivery. Includes commentary on AI happenings filtered through workflow impact. |
| Web Design | `web-design.md` | Website strategy and build process for service businesses. Sites as sales tools, performance as a build-process problem, Figma to dev handoff at agency scale, WordPress/Elementor as the practical agency stack. |
| ClickUp Architecture | `clickup-architecture.md` | ClickUp as a platform. Configuration, rollout, adoption, automations, custom fields, hierarchies, views. Cross-context (agency portfolio management and personal task management). |
| Knowledge Management & Personal Systems | `personal-systems.md` | Second-brain practice, personal knowledge systems, productivity at the individual level. Obsidian, JonathanOS as a second-brain pattern, personal ClickUp, capture-process-refine flows. |

## Pillar discipline

A source can touch multiple pillars. List all that apply at distillation time.

Pillars carry territory and Jonathan's earned authority within it. They do not carry surface-specific format rules (those live in `voice/format-patterns.md` and the `.claude/rules/` files). Each pillar file does include a short "Surface notes" section flagging anything format-specific worth knowing for that territory.
