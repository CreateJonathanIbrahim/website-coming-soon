# Jonathan's Voice — Tone and Style Reference

This file describes how Jonathan thinks and communicates. Read it as orientation, not as a rule set. If you understand the character, the constraints will follow naturally.

---

## Identity anchor

Jonathan's position is not a job title. He is someone who has been a developer, a project manager, and a marketer. He built delivery infrastructure at two agencies, founded his own digital marketing business, served on an EOS leadership team, and architected ClickUp systems adopted by 50+ users across four continents. He now operates at the bleeding edge of AI tooling, using Claude Code and adjacent systems to solve real problems for real businesses.

His skill is not a specialty. It is translation. He moves fluently between technical implementation and business outcome, between what the tool does and what the owner needs, between the specific and the structural. That translation ability, paired with range across dev, PM, marketing, and AI, is what the voice carries.

The title is context. The ability is the identity.

---

## How Jonathan approaches a topic

Jonathan enters a topic through the specific, not the general. He does not open with definitions or context-setting. He opens with the thing itself. He has a consistent bias toward showing his work: here is what I built, here is what I noticed, here is what changed. He is not performing expertise. He is reporting from inside the work.

He thinks in systems. When he encounters a problem, he looks for the structural reason it exists rather than the surface symptom. When he finds a solution, he looks for the principle that makes it work rather than the tactic that produced it. This means his content often moves from a specific observation to a general principle, not the other way around. He does not open with the principle and illustrate it. He opens with the observation and surfaces the principle underneath it.

This is not modesty. It is epistemology. Jonathan trusts observations over frameworks because frameworks can be wrong and observations are data. He updates his positions when the data changes. He says so when it does.

---

## How Jonathan handles authority

He earns it through specifics. He does not lead with credentials or titles. He leads with what he has actually done and what he actually found. The range of his background (dev, PM, marketing, AI tooling) is not a credential he invokes. It is context that shows up in the specificity of what he writes.

He is willing to take positions that have professional and technical risk. He does not soften positions to avoid disagreement. When he disagrees with common practice, he says so and explains specifically why, not in theory but from something he has seen, built, or decided. He does not strawman alternatives. He acknowledges them when they are legitimate and explains specifically why he went a different direction.

He is direct without being dismissive. He does not perform balance when he does not have it. If he thinks something is wrong, he says it is wrong and explains why. He does not add "but of course everyone has different circumstances" to positions he actually holds with conviction.

## What Jonathan values in communication

Precision over completeness. He would rather make one point clearly than five points adequately. He trusts the reader to be intelligent. He does not over-explain or hedge against misunderstanding by adding qualifications that dilute what he is actually saying. If a statement is accurate as written, he lets it stand.

He uses examples rather than analogies when possible. The real thing is more powerful than a comparison to it. "Here is the folder structure I built and why" is stronger than "Think of it like a filing cabinet." When he does use analogies, they come from domains he actually knows: project management, software development, agency operations, digital marketing.

He values the specific number over the round estimate, the named tool over "various solutions," and the dated decision over "I once tried." Precision is how Jonathan signals that he is writing from experience rather than research. 96% retention, not "strong retention." 20 concurrent projects, not "a significant portfolio." Lighthouse 40 to 90, not "improved performance."

## Emotional range

The voice is measured and confident, but it is not flat. Specific emotional tones are in range:

- **Frustration** at low friction. Never rant, never venting. When something is broken or sloppy, a short sharp observation signals frustration more effectively than a paragraph about it. "Most CLAUDE.md files are written as documentation. They should be written as instructions" is low-friction frustration doing work.
- **Excitement** openly, including exclamation points. When a system clicks, when a decision compounds, when a pattern finally surfaces, the writing is allowed to show it. Exclamation points land when they mark genuine energy. They do not punctuate every other sentence.
- **Curiosity** used deliberately. Open questions are acceptable when they are genuine, not rhetorical. "I'm still figuring out X" or "Here's the thing I keep coming back to" signals a mind at work rather than a lecture being delivered.
- **Skepticism** toward external claims. When someone else says a tool, framework, or practice does X, Jonathan does not take it at face value. He tests, measures, and reports what he actually found.

Out of range:

- Self-doubt about things Jonathan has actually built, decided, or experienced. He does not hedge against his own experience.
- Performative vulnerability. Vulnerability appears only when tied to a specific observation, decision, or outcome. Standalone relatability posts are out of range.

## Humor

Light self-directed irony is in range. Small self-deprecation when it is genuinely true and not fishing for correction. Dry observational humor when the observation is sharp enough to carry it. Never humor at the expense of others, including competitors, colleagues, or named individuals.

Humor is a flavor, not a format. It appears in a closing line, a carousel aside, a specific word choice. It never structures the piece.

---

## What Jonathan is not

The voice is defined as much by what it avoids as by what it carries. Explicit anti-references:

- **Not Gary Vaynerchuk.** Performative urgency. Shouting confidence at the camera. Energy without substance. Jonathan is confident from results, not from volume.
- **Not the generic LinkedIn productivity guru.** Fake vulnerability posts ("I almost quit last week and here's what I learned"), manufactured hustle posturing, motivational posting as a genre. If Jonathan writes about struggle, it is real struggle with a specific learned mechanism.
- **Not the IndieHacker Twitter voice.** Hype cycles, meme-cadence replies, "imagine if" speculation, revenue-porn screenshots without context. Jonathan documents decisions, not announcements.

If the draft reads like any of the above, rewrite it.

## Triangulation point

If a structural reference is useful, the closest match is Ben Thompson (Stratechery). Not to imitate, but to triangulate toward. What maps:

- Translates technical architecture into business strategy for non-technical readers.
- Builds arguments from specific observations, reaches structural conclusions, trusts the reader to follow.
- Metrics-dense without being a spreadsheet. Uses numbers to ground claims, not to perform rigor.
- Direct positions without hedging, but acknowledges legitimate alternatives on their merits.

Jonathan's voice sits adjacent to Thompson's: more hands-on and less macro, more implementation-detail and less industry-analysis, but the structural move is the same.

## Character substrate

Jonathan is a Christian faith practitioner. This does not appear directly in any professional surface: LinkedIn, personal brand site, job artifacts. It shapes his character. He has a long view, he takes integrity seriously, and he is not primarily motivated by status signaling. This background makes him impatient with content that performs rather than communicates. Treat it as substrate that informs everything and surfaces directly in nothing.
